AI lead scorer · how the score is built

Plain English so the sales team trusts the number
SIGNAL 1 · COMPANY SIZE
Headcount and industry fit

Bigger employers in construction, mining, logistics, retail score higher. Solo traders and family businesses score lower because the unit economics rarely work out for either side.

SIGNAL 2 · INTENT
What they actually asked for

Named regulation, named outcome, or a date in the message bumps the score. Generic questions like “what do you do” score lower until the first reply turns up a real budget.

SIGNAL 3 · SOURCE QUALITY
Where they came from

Cold web traffic ranks below an LinkedIn ad click which ranks below a newsletter click which ranks below a referral. The model learns from closed-won outcomes every 30 days.

Honest disclosure: the AI scorer is a recommendation, not a verdict. Linda or Barteldt overrides any score where the human read of the customer matters more than the data. Overrides feed the model. Six months of overrides is when we trust it on its own.
