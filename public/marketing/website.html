The 5-stage psychological arc

Every landing page builds in this order · Helena voice + PIF calibration on every word
01
PATTERN INTERRUPT
First fold names the reader. Specific role, specific industry, specific moment. No "Welcome to Care Net." Instead: "Construction HSE leads · the annual SHEQ audit is 12 weeks away."
PIF · Adult ego-state · directness 8
02
PAIN RECOGNITION
The specific situation they keep losing sleep over. Not "OH compliance is complex" but "the inspector arrives and you cannot find Lerato's exit medical from 2023."
PIF · empathy 9 · pace 4
03
AUTHORITY & PROOF
CNC's qualification (OHASA Act 85 of 1993 expert), the four locations, the named-client stories. "Sasol Secunda. 600 staff. Three years zero non-compliance findings."
PIF · social proof 8 · detail 7
04
SPECIFIC OUTCOME
What changes after they sign. Named time savings. "Audit prep drops from 14 days to 3 hours. Lerato's records are one click. The inspector leaves at 11am."
PIF · warmth 7 · autonomy 9
05
SINGLE INVITATION
One CTA. No "learn more." Always action-led. "Book a 30-minute compliance audit." Pre-filled with industry, headcount, location, primary concern.
PIF · directness 7 · empathy 8 · NO urgency
