CHAPTER 01

OUR HISTORY

A mother and daughter started something in 2006 that twenty years later employs a national team and partners with industries across South Africa. The milestones below are the spine of who we are.

2006
Founded as Ani's Medical Care CC
Sister Ani Bakker and her daughter Linda Du Plessis open a small occupational health company.
2012
Rebranded to Care Net Consultants
Formal rebrand, significant growth driven by new occupational-health regulations.
2016
Elandsfontein and Brackenfell branches launch
First multi-site footprint, deepening the Western Cape and Gauteng presence.
2019
Consolidation under Barteldt Bakker
All branches and ownership consolidated. The foundation for the next decade is set.
2020
Port Elizabeth opens, then COVID-19
Port Elizabeth branch launches just before the pandemic. CNC pivots to keep workplaces safe through it.
2024
Tanya Nel returns to lead marketing
Brand re-investment begins. The visual identity you are reading now starts here.
2025
Equity partners join, Project 2030 begins
Tanya Nel and Petrus van Zyl become equity partners. Strategy shifts to digitalise the company into a market-leading SME.
2030
Project 2030 · The ambition
Become the largest SME in South Africa's occupational health sector through digital innovation, telemedicine, mental wellness, and Occupational Health Pods.
"To revolutionise the occupational health industry by integrating cutting-edge technology and ethical, safe, efficient, innovative practices."

CHAPTER 02

VISION & DNA

We aim to transform occupational health services across Africa through digital innovation, providing accessible, affordable care through our network of Occupational Health Pods. Our focus on telemedicine, mental wellness, and health education, especially in price-sensitive markets like industrial, manufacturing, and construction, will ensure we reach those who need us. By fostering shared value through strategic partnerships, we aim to become the largest SME in South Africa's occupational health sector.

CHAPTER 09

VOICE

Professional, compassionate, innovative. Clear, informative, supportive. Speak in simple language, adult-to-adult. Avoid jargon, avoid hype, never lecture.

CHAPTER 10

APPROVAL

Every asset that leaves the Studio is routed for approval based on its content. Clinical claims go to the OMP. Nursing-led copy goes to the OHNP. Brand and digital assets go to the Director. Template-safe items (birthday cards, internal announcements) auto-approve.
